Axis:
Horizontal - A/D, Left/Right, (Left Stick Horizontal), (D-Pad Horizontal)
Vertical - W/S, Up/Down, (Left Stick Vertical), (D-Pad Vertical)

Menu Buttons:
Submit - Space, Enter, Return, (A)
Cancel - Escape, (B)
Menu - Escape, (+)/(Menu)

Game Buttons:
Jump - Space, (A)
Shadow - F, (Left Bumper)
Pickup - C, (Y)
Interact - E, (B)
Dash - Left Click, (Right Bumper)
Attack - Right Click, (X)
Show Lines - Q, (-)/(View)/(Back)
